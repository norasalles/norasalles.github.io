1.Live Connect Interface          
1.1.Live Connect Login Post        
  Live Connect navigates to the supplier’s web booking site and submits a form post that contains the agent’s login information and the traveler’s information.
 
  Information passed by ClientBase Plus to a Live Connect provider allows automatic login of an Agent to the Live Connect provider’s website and auto fill of passenger and billing information when in the reservation process.
