Agency Details

Path: Agency > Agency Details

This help topic shows you how to configure the following agency information:

  • Contact information
  • E-Mail addresses
  • Office hours

Notes   

  • You must be an Agency Superuser or above to make entries to agency details.
  • Since it is used in so many places throughout the system, it is extremely important that you make sure the agency information is accurate and up to date.

For more information, click one of the following:

Contact Information

Path: Agency > Agency Details | Contact Information tab

This page controls contact information for the host travel agency. The information entered on this page is used throughout the system for ticketing, reservations, queries to the GDS and more. Users will typically see this information on the Review Trip page and Confirmation page.

To enter contact Information

  1. Enter information into the following fields:

    Field Label

    Enter or select the...

    Agency Name

    Name of the host travel agency used by your company.

    The name entered displays as “from” name and in the body of confirmation e-mails. It also appears in the Travel Agency Information section of the Reservation Complete and Trip Details pages if you have enabled the Display travel agency information toggle on the Setup > Site Preferences > Itinerary Settings page.

    If the agency address is not listed in the Exclude Fields section in the PNR Editor, this name is used in the agency address field of PNRs.

    ARC Number

    ARC number used by your host travel agency. This number is currently informational only and does not impact the system settings in any way.

    IATA Number

    IATA number used by your host travel agency. This number is appended to all car and hotel booking segments as the booking source.

    DK Number

    Back office account number for the customer used by this host travel agency. This field can be left blank if it is not applicable or if you are going to create conditional DK numbers in the PNR Editor.

    For Sabre sites only: If the DK number is not listed in the Exclude Fields section in PNR Editor, this DK number is entered into the DK field of all PNRs.

    Contact Person

    Name of a contact person from the host travel agency.

    The agency contact name displays in the body of confirmations e-mails.

    Title

    Title of the contact person from the host travel agency.

    This text is informational only and does not display in e-mails or on the booking site.

    Address 1

    Mailing address for the host travel agency.

    This information appears in the Travel Agency Information section of the Reservation Complete and Trip Details pages if you have enabled the Display travel agency information toggle on the Setup > Site Preferences > Itinerary Settings page.

    If the agency address is not listed in the Exclude Fields section in the PNR Editor, this name is used in the agency address field of PNRs.

    Address 2

    Additional address information of the travel agency.

    This information appears in the Travel Agency Information section of the Reservation Complete and Trip Details pages if you have enabled the Display travel agency information toggle on the Setup > Site Preferences > Itinerary Settings page.

    City

    City in which the host travel agency is located.

    The city name displays in the body of confirmation e-mails. It also appears in the Travel Agency Information section of the Reservation Complete and Trip Details pages if you have enabled the Display travel agency information toggle on the Setup > Site Preferences > Itinerary Settings page.

    If the agency address is not listed in the Exclude Fields section in the PNR Editor, this name is used in the agency address field of PNRs.

    State

    State in which the host travel agency is located.

    The state name displays in the body of confirmation e-mails. It also appears in the Travel Agency Information section of the Reservation Complete and Trip Details pages if you have enabled the Display travel agency information toggle on the Setup > Site Preferences > Itinerary Settings page.

    If the agency address is not listed in the Exclude Fields section in the PNR Editor, this name is used in the agency address field of PNRs.

    Zip Code

    Zip code in which the host travel agency is located.

    The zip code name appears in the Travel Agency Information section of the Reservation Complete and Trip Details pages if you have enabled the Display travel agency information toggle on the Setup > Site Preferences > Itinerary Settings page.

    If the agency address is not listed in the Exclude Fields section in the PNR Editor, this name is used in the agency address field of PNRs.

    Country

    Country for the host travel agency.

    The country name displays in the body of confirmation e-mails. It also appears in the Travel Agency Information section of the Reservation Complete and Trip Details pages if you have enabled the Display travel agency information toggle on the Setup > Site Preferences > Itinerary Settings page.

    Phone Number

    General phone number for the host travel agency.

    The phone number displays in the body of confirmation e-mails. It also appears in the Travel Agency Information section of the Reservation Complete and Trip Details pages if you have enabled the Display travel agency information toggle on the Setup > Site Preferences > Itinerary Settings page.

    Fax Number

    Relevant fax number for the host travel agency.

    The fax number displays in the body of confirmation e-mails. It also appears in the Travel Agency Information section of the Reservation Complete and Trip Details pages if you have enabled the Display travel agency information toggle on the Setup > Site Preferences > Itinerary Settings page.

    Toll Free Number

    Toll-free phone number for the host travel agency.

    The toll-free number displays in the Travel Agency Information section of the Reservation Complete and Trip Details pages if you have enabled the Display travel agency information toggle on the Setup > Site Preferences > Itinerary Settings page.

    Normal hours of operation

    Hours of operation that appears with information about the host travel agency. This text field supports HTML.

    The time entered in this field is for display purposes to users and will not affect system settings in any way.

    The numbers display in the Travel Agency Information section of the Reservation Complete and Trip Details pages if you have enabled the Display travel agency information toggle on the Setup > Site Preferences > Itinerary Settings page.

    Additional information about your agency

    Enter additional information in text or HTML. This information is displayed to users on the Itinerary Review and Confirmation pages.
  2. Click Save.

E-mail Addresses

Path: Agency > Agency Details | E-mail Addresses tab

To enter e-mail addresses

  1. Select whether to Send PNR confirmations to the agency. When you enable this field, the agency receives an abbreviated version of the confirmation e-mails sent to travelers.
  2. Enter the Agency E-mail address for PNR confirmation notifications. You can enter multiple e-mail addresses by separating the entries with a comma (,) or a semi-colon (;).
  3. Configure confirmation e-mails sent to users:

    1. Enter the PNR confirmation From e-mail address.

      Note  The default address is pnr-notification@getthere.net. The from address on the e-mails consists of the word PNR, followed by the agency name and the e-mail address entered into this field. Example: PNR-ABC Travel [pnr-notification@getthere.net]

    2. Enter the PNR confirmation Reply-to e-mail address.

      Note  The default address is pnr-notification@getthere.net. GetThere does not respond to e-mails received.

    3. Enter the E-mail address that will receive updates when the system encounters preferred hotel properties that do not have your preferred rates loaded in the host GDS. You can enter multiple e-mail addresses by separating the entries with a comma (,) or a semi-colon (;).
    4. Enter the E-mail address that will receive updates when users change their online profile. These notifications are sent when a user or a travel arranger changes the profile. The e-mail does not contain all of the data fields contained in the profile. You can enter multiple e-mail addresses by separating the entries with a comma (,) or a semi-colon (;).
  4. Note that the system displays the GDS System for the site.
  5. Enter the Primary GDS Pseudo City Code that supports this booking site.
  6. Enter the Primary GDS Queue, in the above Pseudo City Code, where the system will place all new, changed and cancelled PNR's.
  7. Click Save.

Office Hours Configuration

Path: Agency > Agency Details | Office Hours Configuration tab

This page specifies the business hours during which ticketing and other normal business functions are performed.  

To set up the agency office hours

  1. Enter the time difference between the server time and the current time for this office in + hours or - hours.
  2. Select the Day of Week Settings as follows
    1. Select one or more days of week in a Day of Week list for which you want to specify business hours. To select multiple items, hold down the Ctrl key while you click other items that you want to select.
    2. Select the Opening Time and Close Time for days you specified in the Day of Week list.

  3. Specify up to ten custom Holidays for your agency.

    Note  The system does not contain default holidays. You need to explicitly define and update holidays every year.

    For each holiday, do the following:

    1. In the Name box, type the holiday name.
    2. In the Start Time list, click the time your office is closes for the holiday.
    3. In the Date, Month, and Year lists, click the date your office is closes for the holiday.
    4. In the End Time list, click the time your office opens again after the holiday.
    5. In the Date, Month, and Year lists, click the date your office opens again after the holiday.

    For example, if Memorial Day falls on Monday May 26 and your office closes for the entire weekend, enter May 23, 2008 at 5:00 PM as the Start Time and May 27, 2008 at 6:30 AM as the End Time

  4. Click Save.
