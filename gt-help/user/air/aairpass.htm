AAirpass

Path: Air > AAirpass

AAirpass is an American Airlines travel program (Sabre only) where companies pre-pay for travel at a negotiated rate per mile for a specified term. When travelers purchase AAirpass flights, the flight mileage is deducted from the AAirpass account.

Notes   

  • If you enable AAirpass, you also need to set up and maintain your company's AAirpass agreements.
  • If you enable AAirpass, be sure to set the maximum number of travelers to one.

To enable AAirpass

  1. To turn AAirpass on for your site, click Enable AAirpass.
  2. In the AAirpass Fare Display list, select the way AAirpass fares are presented on your site as follows:

  3. Click Save Changes.

To disable AAirpass

  1. To turn AAirpass off on your site, click Disable AAirpass.

AAirpass Zones

An AAirpass zone consists of the zone name and the countries included in the zone. You use these zones to modify your AAirpass zone agreements and apply mileage premiums to flights within the zone.

Note  You can only associate a country with one zone. Once you have added a country to a zone, the name is automatically removed from the Unassigned Countries list and cannot be used in another zone.

To add a zone

  1. Click the Configure Zones link.
  2. Click the Add New Zone button.
  3. In the Zone Name box, type a name for the zone.
  4. In the Unassigned Countries list, select the countries you want to add to the Assigned Countries list, and then click Add. To select multiple items, hold down the Ctrl key while you click other items that you want to select.
  5. Click Save.

To edit a zone

  1. Click the Configure Zones link.
  2. Click the Edit button in the row for the zone you want to modify.
  3. Do one of the following:

    To

    Do

    Add countries to the zone

    1. In the Unassigned Countries list, select the countries you want to add to the Assigned Countries list. To select multiple items, hold down the Ctrl key while you click other items that you want to select.
    2. Click Add.

    Remove countries from the zone

    1. In the Assigned Countries list, select the countries you want to remove. To select multiple items, hold down the Ctrl key while you click other items that you want to select.
    2. Click Remove.
  4. Click Save.

To delete a zone

  1. Click the Configure Zones link.
  2. Click Delete.
  3. To confirm that you want to delete this agreement, click OK.

AAirpass Agreements

The basic AAirpass Zone Agreement consists of the term of the agreement (the period between the effective date and the end date) and the rate per mile (in USD) that is applied to applicable flights during the period. The total fare for an eligible zone or market agreement AAirpass itinerary is calculated as follows: Airpass fare = Itinerary mileage X (mileage premium X rate per mile)

  • Zone Agreements – Zone agreements use zone modifiers, which apply a mileage premium percentage when travel occurs within the countries in the zone and when the number of travel miles is within the range defined in the modifier. To successfully create a zone agreement, you must first create a zone (Configure Zones).
  • Market Agreements – Market agreements use market modifiers, which are applied to specific markets (airport to airport, zone to airport, or country to airport). You can configure directional or non-directional modifiers and apply a mileage premium (a percentage) when travel occurs between the defined markets based on calculated mileage.

To create an agreement

  1. Click the Configure AAirpass Agreements link.
  2. Click Add Agreement.
  3. Enter the agreement start date in the Effective Date field and the completion date in the End Date field. (An agreements date range cannot overlap the date range of another agreement.)
  4. Enter the rate to be charged per mile (in U.S. dollars) in the Rate Per Mile field.
  5. Click Save to record your settings (or Cancel to collapse the option and exit without saving your changes).

To edit an agreement:

  1. Click the Configure AAirpass Agreements link.
  2. Click Edit.
  3. Make the modifications you desire (you cannot change the agreement type).
  4. Click Save to record your settings (or Cancel to collapse the option and exit without saving your changes).

To delete an agreement:

  1. Click the Configure AAirpass Agreements link.
  2. Click Delete.
  3. To confirm that you want to delete this agreement, click OK.

Zone Modifiers

A Zone Modifier is applied to an applicable itinerary when travel occurs within the countries defined in the zone and when the number of miles is within the specified mileage range. The system multiplies the mileage premium for the applicable class of service by the agreement's rate per mile to determine the effective rate per mile.

To create a modifier

  1. If necessary, click the Configure AAirpass Agreements link.
  2. In the Modifiers column, click Add Modifiers.
  3. In the Zone Name list, click the zone to which this modifier applies.
  4. Enter the mileage range for which the modifier applies as follows:
    1. In the From box, type the lowest mileage within the zone to which the modifier will apply.
    2. In the To box, type the highest mileage within the zone that the modifier will apply.

    Note  This mileage range cannot overlap the mileage range of another zone modifier.

  5. For each class of service in the Mileage Premiums column, type the amount by which the mileage should be multiplied. To calculate the AAirpass fare, the system multiplies this result by the rate per mile and calculates the percentage.
  6. Click Save.

To view or edit a modifier:

  1. If necessary, click the Configure AAirpass Agreements link.
  2. If necessary, in the Modifiers column, click Add Modifiers.
  3. If necessary, click Edit in the row for the modifier you want to change, and edit the modifier.
  4. Click Save.

To delete a modifier:

  1. If necessary, click the Configure AAirpass Agreements link.
  2. In the row for the modifier you want to change, click Delete.
  3. To confirm that you want to delete this modifier, click OK.
