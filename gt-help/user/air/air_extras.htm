Air Extras

Path: Air > Air Extras

For all sites where Sabre is not the primary GDS, you can use the Air Extras page to enable or disable Air Extras.

To configure Air Extras for a site

  1. To enable Air Extras for the site, select the Enable Air Extras check box.
  2. Click Save.
