Car Rates/Discounts (Direct Sell Administration)

Path: Car > Car Rates/Discounts

You can use the Direct Sell Administration page to review, edit, or delete an existing car rental contracts from companies that are contracted and available to book on your site using Direct Sell.

Add a Rental Contract

  1. Click the Add a new contract region link.
  2. Select the car rental company for this contract.
  3. Enter the Negotiated Discount Number specified by the car rental company.

  4. Indicate whether to Always return the lowest rate, even if it is less than the negotiated rate. If you click No, only the negotiated rate displays even if there are lower rates.

    Note  If the site is set to book only the contracted rates, click No.

  5. In the Vendor Preference list, click the unique preference for this vendor based on importance. This sets the vendor preference for Express Booking when the selection criteria is set to Most preferred contracted vendor.
  6. (Optional) Enter a short title for this Rental Contract.

  7. (Optional) In the Rate Covered Areas boxes, type the rental zones to which the rental rates apply.

  8. Click Save.

    The new contract appears on the Direct Sell Administration page.

Edit a Rental Contract

  1. In the table, click the link showing the short title/description of the rental contract you want to modify.
  2. Change the rental contract information as needed.
  3. Click Save.

Delete a Rental Contract

  1. In the table, click the link showing the short title/description of the rental contract you want to delete.

  2. Click Delete.

    The row for the rental contract disappears from the table.
