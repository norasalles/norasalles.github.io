Trip Approvals

Path: Policy > Corporate Travel Policy > Trip Approvals

If Travel Policy system behavior is set to Select from a list of travel authorizers when users select out-of-policy itineraries, they are required to select an authorizer who is notified. When the traveler completes their booking, the selected authorizer receives an e-mail containing the traveler's full itinerary and the reason selected by the traveler to explain their need to deviate from their assigned travel policy. The travel authorizer does not need to take any action for the itinerary to be ticketed. The travel authorizer can decline this itinerary by responding to the e-mail, which is automatically sent to the Host Travel Agency.

Configure Trip Approvals

  1. In the E-mail Header Message box, type a header message that is included in the e-mail that is sent to the travel policy approver.
  2. In the Travel Authorizer Name boxes, type the names of the travel authorizers.
  3. In the Authorizer E-mail Address boxes, type the e-mail addresses of the travel authorizers.
  4. Click Save.
