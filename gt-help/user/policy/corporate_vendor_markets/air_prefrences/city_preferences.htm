City Preferences

Path: Policy > Corporate Vendor Markets> Air Preferences - City Preferences

City/Airport Market Preferences form part of a hierarchy of airline preferences for your site. City/Airport Market Preferences are applied to any matching itinerary and take precedence over your Country Market Based Preferences, Global Market Based Preferences, and Regional Market Based Preferences.

You can use the Airline City/Airport Market Preference Administration page to enter up to 10 airline preferences and up to 6 airline exclusions that are applied to any matching airline itinerary. Airline preference entries are sensitive to primary, secondary, and tertiary rank. Configure the display of Preferred Airline Vendors on the Air and Car Setup page.

To add a city/airport preference

  1. Click the Add a New Preference link.

    The Airline City/Airport Market Based Preference Editor page appears.

  2. In the City/Airport Assignment 1 list, type the first city or airport for your preference.

    Note  If you want to setup the preference to affect flights going in and out of this city/airport from and to any other city/airport, you should not enter the second city/airport.

  3. If you want to specify a second city/airport so that your preference affects flights going from one city/airport to another, in the City/Airport Assignment 2 list, type the second city/airport.

    Note  Many Global Distribution Systems (GDS) return alternate airports for cities that have multiple airports. If you want to specify that this preference applies to a specific airport enter an “=” after the city. For example, if you want to specify New York LaGuardia airport only you enter "LGA=".

  4. Ignore CIty/Airport Substitutions as it is no longer supported.

  5. In the Preferred Airline Rank boxes, type the two-letter airline codes for the preferred airline vendors.

    Note  The Period Targeted Share, Period Actual Share, Period Start Date, Period End Date are no longer supported.

  6. Enter the two letter codes of the airlines to be demoted (up to 6).

  7. Indicate Should demoted airlines be displayed to users?

    Note  Demoted airlines may not work consistently when configured in Market Based Preferences only. Contact your GetThere representative to have them demoted through GetThere Administration as well as through site administration Market Based Preferences.

  8. Indicate whether market preferences are bi-directional. If you click Yes, the system looks for flights that come to or from either of the two cities/airports specified above. If you click No, the system only looks for flights that start in City/Airport Assignment 1 and end in City/Airport Assignment 2.
  9. Indicate whether Air Connect fares be displayed for this market preference.
  10. Click Save.

To edit a city/airport preference

  1. In the table, click the link in Market column for the city/airport preference you want to edit.

    The Airline City/Airport Market Based Preference Editor page appears.

  2. Change the information as needed.
  3. Click Save.

Delete a city/airport preference

  1. In the table, click the link in Market column for the city/airport preference you want to delete.

    The Airline City/Airport Market Based Preference Editor page appears.

  2. Click Delete.

    The Airline City/Airport Market Preference Administration page appears again showing any remaining city/airport preferences.
