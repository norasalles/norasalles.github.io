Country Preferences

Path: Policy > Corporate Vendor Markets> Air Preferences - Country Preferences

Country Market Based Preferences form part of a hierarchy of airline preferences for your site. Country Airline Market Preferences are applied to any matching itinerary that does not have previously entered City/Airport Airline Market Based Preferences. Airline itineraries that match a Country Market Based Preference will take precedence over your Global Market Based Preferences and Regional Market Based Preferences.

You can use the Airline Country Market Preference Administration page to enter up to 10 airline preferences and up to 6 airline exclusions that are applied to any matching airline itinerary that does not match a previously entered City/Airport Airline Market Based Preference. Airline preference entries are sensitive to primary, secondary, and tertiary rank. Configure the display of Preferred Airline Vendors on the Air and Car Setup page.

To add a country preference

  1. Click the Add a New Preference link.

    The Airline Country Market Based Preference Editor page appears.

  2. In the Country Assignment 1 list, type the two-letter code for the first country for your preference.

    Note  If you want to setup the preference to affect flights going in and out of this country from and to any other country, you should not enter the second country.

  3. If you want to specify a second country so that your preference affects flights going from one country to another, in the Country Assignment 2 list, type the two-letter code for the second country.

  4. In the Preferred Airline Rank boxes, type the two-letter airline codes for the preferred airline vendors.

    Notes   

    • These settings are overridden by City/Airport preferences.
    • The Period Targeted Share, Period Actual Share, Period Start Date, Period End Date are no longer supported.
  5. Enter the two letter codes of the airlines to be demoted (up to 6).

  6. Indicate Should demoted airlines be displayed to users?

    Note  Demoted airlines may not work consistently when configured in Market Based Preferences only. Contact your GetThere representative to have them demoted through GetThere Administration as well as through site administration Market Based Preferences.

  7. Indicate whether market preferences are bi-directional. If you click Yes, the system looks for flights that come to or from either of the two countries specified above. If you click No, the system only looks for flights that start in Country Assignment 1 and end in Country Assignment 2.
  8. Indicate whether Air Connect fares be displayed for this market preference.
  9. Click Save.

To edit a country preference

  1. In the table, click the link in Market column for the country preference you want to edit.

    The Airline Country Market Based Preference Editor page appears.

  2. Change the information as needed.
  3. Click Save.

Delete a country preference

  1. In the table, click the link in Market column for the country preference you want to delete.

    The Airline Country Market Based Preference Editor page appears.

  2. Click Delete.

    The Airline Country Market Preference Administration page appears again showing any remaining country preferences.
