Global Preferences

Path: Policy > Corporate Vendor Markets> Air Preferences - Global Preferences

Global Market Based Preferences form the base to a hierarchy of airline preferences for your site. Global Airline Market Preferences are applied to any matching itinerary that does not have previously entered Region Airline Market Preferences, Country Airline Market Preferences, or City/Airport Airline Market Based Preferences.

You can use the Airline Global Market Preference Administration page to enter up to 10 airline preferences and up to 6 airline exclusions that are applied to any matching airline itinerary that does not match a previously entered Region, Country or City/Airport Airline Market Based Preference. Airline preference entries are sensitive to primary, secondary, and tertiary rank. Configure the display of Preferred Airline Vendors on the Air and Car Setup page.

To add a global preference

  1. Click the Add a New Preference link.

    The Airline Global Market Based Preference Editor page appears.

  2. In the Preferred Airline Rank boxes, type the two-letter airline codes for the preferred airline vendors.

    Notes   

    • These settings are overridden by Region, Country, or City/Airport preferences.
    • The Period Targeted Share, Period Actual Share, Period Start Date, Period End Date are no longer supported.
  3. Enter the two letter codes of the airlines to be demoted (up to 6).

  4. Indicate Should demoted airlines be displayed to users?

    Note  Demoted airlines may not work consistently when configured in Market Based Preferences only. Contact your GetThere representative to have them demoted through GetThere Administration as well as through site administration Market Based Preferences.

  5. Click Save.

To edit a global preference

  1. In the table, click the link in number in the first column for the global preference you want to edit.

    The Airline Global Market Based Preference Editor page appears.

  2. Change the information as needed.
  3. Click Save.

Delete a global preference

  1. In the table, click the link in number in the first column for the global preference you want to delete.

    The Airline Global Market Based Preference Editor page appears.

  2. Click Delete.

    The Airline Global Market Preference Administration page appears again showing no global preferences.
