Regional Preferences

Path: Policy > Corporate Vendor Markets> Air Preferences - Regional Preferences

Regional Market Based Preferences form part of a hierarchy of airline preferences for your site. Regional Airline Market Preferences are applied to any matching itinerary that does not have previously entered Country Airline Market Preferences or City/Airport Airline Market Based Preferences. Airline itineraries that match a Regional Market Based Preference will take precedence over your Global Market Based Preferences.

You can use the Airline Regional Market Preference Administration page to enter up to 10 airline preferences and up to six airline exclusions that are applied to any matching airline itinerary that does not match a previously entered Country or City/Airport Airline Market Based Preference. Airline preference entries are sensitive to primary, secondary, and tertiary rank. Configure the display of Preferred Airline Vendors on the Air and Car Setup page.

Prior to setting regional preferences, you must first create your regions. Regions can be any countries which you group together and assign a name. Preferred flights operating to, from, or between any airport in the countries that are defined in region assignments 1 and 2 are highlighted. For more information, see Geographic Area Editor.

To add a regional preference

  1. Click the Add a New Preference link.

    The Airline Regional Market Based Preference Editor page appears.

  2. In the Region Assignment 1 list, click the first region for your preference.

    Note  If you want to setup the preference to affect flights going in and out of this region from and to any other region, you should not enter the second region.

  3. If you want to specify a second region so that your preference affects flights going from one region to another, in the Region Assignment 2 list, click the second region.

  4. In the Preferred Airline Rank boxes, type the two-letter airline codes for the preferred airline vendors.

    Notes   

    • These settings are overridden by Country or City/Airport preferences.
    • The Period Targeted Share, Period Actual Share, Period Start Date, Period End Date are no longer supported.
  5. Enter the two letter codes of the airlines to be demoted (up to 6).

  6. Indicate Should demoted airlines be displayed to users?

    Note  Demoted airlines may not work consistently when configured in Market Based Preferences only. Contact your GetThere representative to have them demoted through GetThere Administration as well as through site administration Market Based Preferences.

  7. Indicate whether market preferences are bi-directional. If you click Yes, the system looks for flights that come to or from either of the two regions specified above. If you click No, the system only looks for flights that start in Region Assignment 1 and end in Region Assignment 2.
  8. Indicate whether Air Connect fares be displayed for this market preference.
  9. Click Save.

To edit a regional preference

  1. In the table, click the link in Market column for the regional preference you want to edit.

    The Airline Regional Market Based Preference Editor page appears.

  2. Change the information as needed.
  3. Click Save.

Delete a regional preference

  1. In the table, click the link in Market column for the regional preference you want to delete.

    The Airline Regional Market Based Preference Editor page appears.

  2. Click Delete.

    The Airline Regional Market Preference Administration page appears again showing any remaining regional preferences.
