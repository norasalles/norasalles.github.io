Car Market Preferences

Path: Policy > Corporate Vendor Markets> Car Preferences - Car Market Preferences

Car Market Preferences are applied to any matching itinerary that does not have previously entered preferences. Car preferences are setup on the same page for city/airport, state, continental United States, country, and global markets. If you have only one preferred car rental company for your global users, then it is only necessary to create a Global Market for that one car rental company. More specific preferences can also be created.

You can use the Car Rental Market Preference Administration page to enter up to 3 car rental preferences and up to 6 car rental exclusions that are applied to any matching car itinerary. You can configure the display of Preferred Airline Vendors on the Air and Car Setup page.

GetThere supports up to 2,000 car rental market based preferences for each booking site, which can be defined for airport/city, state, continental United States, country, and global markets.

To add a preference

  1. Click the Add a New Preference link.

    The Car Rental Market Based Preference Editor page appears.

  2. In the Location Assignment box, enter the location as follows:

    Hierarchy

    Location Assignment

    Examples

    City

    Enter the three-letter code for an airport servicing the car rental pick-up city.

    This applies to all car rental itineraries that are picked-up at the airport location.

    Note  Multiple airport entries can be entered if separated by a comma

    ORD, SAN

    State

    Enter a two-letter state code for any US state preceded by the letters "US".

    All car rental itineraries that are picked-up at an airport located within the boundaries of the specified state are subject to this set of market preferences.

    Note  Multiple state entries can be entered if separated by a comma

    USTX, USOH

    Continental United States

    Enter "US48"

    All car rental itineraries that are picked-up at any airport within the 48 continental United States are subject to this set of market preferences.

    US48

    Country

    Enter a two-letter country code.

    All car rental itineraries that are picked-up at any airport within the borders of the specified country are subject to this set of market preferences.

    Note  Multiple country entries can be entered if separated by a comma

    FR,UK

    Global

    Enter "Worldwide"

    All car rental itineraries that are picked-up at any airport are subject to this set of market preferences.

    Worldwide

  3. In the Car Rental Market Preferences boxes, type the two-letter car rental codes for the preferred vendors.

    Notes   

    • These settings are overridden by Region, Country, or City/Airport preferences.
    • The Period Targeted Share, Period Actual Share, Period Start Date, Period End Date are no longer supported.
  4. Enter the two-letter codes for What car rental companies are excluded from the availability display?
  5. Click Save.

To edit a global preference

  1. In the table, click the link in the Market column for the preference you want to edit.

    The Car Rental Market Based Preference Editor page appears.

  2. Change the information as needed.
  3. Click Save.

Delete a global preference

  1. In the table, click the link in number in the first column for the preference you want to delete.

    The Car Rental Market Based Preference Editor page appears.

  2. Click Delete.

    The Car Rental Market Preference Administration appears again showing any remaining preferences.
