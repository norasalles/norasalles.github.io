Global Preferences

Path: Policy > Corporate Vendor Markets> Hotel Preferences - Global Preferences

Global Market Based Preferences form the base to a hierarchy of hotel preferences for your site. Global Hotel Market Preferences are applied to any matching itinerary that does not have previously entered Region Hotel Market Preferences, Country Hotel Market Preferences, or City/Airport Hotel Market Based Preferences.

You can use the Hotel Global Market Preference Administration page to enter up to 10 hotel preferences and up to six hotel exclusions that are applied to any matching hotel itinerary that does not match a previously entered Regional, Country, or City/Airport Hotel Market Based Preference.

Note  Market Based Preferences for hotels are applied on a chain level, not on an individual hotel property basis. GetThere supports up to 2,000 hotel chain market based preferences for each booking site, which can be defined for airport, city, country, region and global markets

To add a global preference

  1. Click Add.

    The Add new Hotel Chain Global Market Based Preference dialog box appears.

  2. In the Hotel Chain And Channel Market Preferences boxes, type the two-letter hotel chain code of your preferred hotels.

    Note  You can enter the two-letter hotel brand codes to designate all hotel chains within that brand at the preferred level.

  3. In the What hotel chains are excluded boxes, type up to 10 hotel chains you want to exclude from the global preferences.

    Note  You can enter the two-letter hotel brand codes to exclude all hotel chains within that brand.

  4. Indicate whether to Display excluded hotel chains to the users.
  5. If you have Hotel Connect enabled, do the following:
    1. Indicate whether to Display Hotel Connect rates to users.
    2. If you selected the previous check box, indicate whether to Only display Hotel Connect properties in search results.
    3. Select the Hotel Connect channels from which you want to display hotels.
  6. If you selected to enable Hotel Connect channels, do the following:
    1. In the Hotel Connect Channels area, indicate whether to Display Hotel Connect rates to users.
    2. If you select to display Hotel Connect rates, do the following:
      1. Indicate whether to Only display Hotel Connect properties in search results.
      2. In the list on the left, click the Hotel Connect channels you want to use, and click Add.

  7. Click Save.

  8. If you selected to enable Hotel Connect channels, do the following:

    1. In the Hotel Connect Channels column, click the Configure Channels link.

      The Configure Hotel Connect Channels page appears.

    2. For each channel, do the following:
      1. In the Supplier Parameters column, click the Configure link.
      2. Indicate which account type should be used to book on the external site? as follows:

        To

        Do

        Send user name, password, and other values from the GetThere database to Travelfusion regardless of what is entered in the fields below

        Click Always use GetThere Default.

        Send user name, password, and other values from the fields below to Travelfusion instead of what is in the GetThere database

        Click Always use Agency Default.

      3. If necessary, enter information in user name, password, or other fields shown.
  9. Click Save.

To edit a global preference

  1. In the table, click the link in number in the first column for the global preference you want to edit.

    The Modify Hotel Chain Global Market Based Preference dialog box appears.

  2. Change the information as needed.
  3. Click Save.

Delete a global preference

  1. Select the check boxes for the global preferences you want to delete.

  2. Click Delete.

    A confirmation dialog box appears.

  3. Click OK.

Delete all global preferences

  1. Select the check box in the header row.

  2. Click Delete.

    A confirmation dialog box appears.

  3. Click OK.
