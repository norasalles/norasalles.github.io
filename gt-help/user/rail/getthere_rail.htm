GetThere Rail

Path: Rail > GetThere Rail

GetThere Rail is the newly branded name for Rail Connect. GetThere Rail is now integrated with the Sabre Rail Consolidator Platform for Amtrak. Your selections on the GetThere Rail Super User page determines which of the following you are using:

  • RailConnect (a.k.a. GetThere Rail – You can use this tab to configure the connection between GetThere and the Sabre Rail Consolidator Platform for Amtrak.
  • SabreRail – You can use this tab to configure the legacy connection to Amtrak and connect to Trainline.

Note  You should only enable RailConnect (a.k.a. GetThere Rail) or SabreRail, not both.

You can use GetThere Rail to shop, book, and cancel rail bookings for Amtrak, Trainline, and Via Rail. If you are using Sabre Rail, you can only use Amtrak or Trainline. You can compare corporate rates, rates for discount cards, and published rates across multiple classes of service for a single train option. You can compare the rules specific to the fares for each train option.

Notes   

Note  For Canadian sites, on the Localization page in the Miscellaneous area, in the What display standard should other display items follow? list, click US standard displays.

Note  For Trainline, the Discount Card tab are not supported.

For more information, click one of the following:

Setup GetThere Rail Vendors

  1. Click the Setup tab.
  2. To enable RailConnect settings for this site, select the Enable GetThere Rail check box. You cannot enable GetThere Rail for a subsite unless it is enabled for the main site.
  3. Do one of the following:

    To

    Do

    Add a GetThere Rail vendor

    Note  If you are using Sabre Rail, you can only add Amtrak.

    1. Click Add. The Add RailConnect Channel page appears.

    2. In the Channels list, click the rail vendor you want to add.

      Notes   

      • If you are using GetThere Rail, all the vendors are appended with "RailConnect".
      • If you are using Sabre Rail, you can only add Amtrak or Trainline.
    3. Click Save. The vendor appears in the Enabled Channel table, and a message appears stating that the system has saved your changes.

    Delete one or more RailConnect vendors

    1. In the Enabled Channel table, select the vendors you want to delete.
    2. Click Delete. A confirmation message appears.
    3. Click OK. The vendors disappear from the Enabled Channel table, and a message appears stating that the system has saved your changes.

  4. To enable book only functionality (booking the seat but not paying or ticketing at the time of booking), do the following:

    Notes   

    • The agency and/or traveler must complete the ticketing and payment after the booking is complete.
    • This only applies to Amtrak.

    1. Click the Configure link.

      The Configure page appears.

    2. Select the Enable Book Only check box.
    3. In the Agency E-mail box, type the e-mail to which you want notifications sent for the bookings. The Agency can then use the information to ticket and/or make payment for the booking. Based on the Rail supplier's e-mail policy, this e-mail address along with the travelers e-mail address is notified of various events, such as e-ticket generation and refunds on cancellation.
    4. Click Save.

Configure Corporate Discounts

  1. Click the Corporate Discount tab.
  2. Do one of the following:

    To

    Do

    Add a corporate discount

    1. Click Add. The Add Corporate Discount page appears.
    2. In the Program list, click the discount program you want to add.
    3. In the Corporate Discount box, type the account/member number for the discount program.
    4. Click Save. The program appears in the Enabled Corporate Discount table, and a message appears stating that the system has saved your changes.

    Change the account/member number for a corporate discount

    1. In the Enabled Corporate Discount table, click the program name for which you want to change the account/member number. The Modify Corporate Discount page appears.
    2. In the Corporate Discount box, change the account/member number for the discount program.
    3. Click Save. A message appears stating that the system has saved your changes.

    Delete one or more corporate discount

    1. In the Enabled Corporate Discount table, select the programs you want to delete.
    2. Click Delete. A confirmation message appears.
    3. Click OK. The programs disappear from the Enabled Corporate Discount table, and a message appears stating that the system has saved your changes.

Configure Rail Cards

  1. Click the Discount Card tab.
  2. Do one of the following:

    To

    Do

    Activate one or more rail cards

    1. Click a rail card in the list on the left. To select more than one rail card, hold down the Ctrl key while you click other items that you want to select.
    2. Click Add. The rail cards move to the list on the right.

    Deactivate one or more rail cards

    1. Click a rail card in the list on the right. To select more than one rail card, hold down the Ctrl key while you click other items that you want to select.
    2. Click Remove. The rail cards move to the list on the left.
  3. Click Save. A message appears stating that the system has saved your changes.
