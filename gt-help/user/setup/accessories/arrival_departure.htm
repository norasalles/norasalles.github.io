Arrival & Departure Status

Path: Setup > Accessories > Arrival & Departure Status

Arrival & Departure Status is an accessory displayed on the login and/or Home page of the booking tool that is used to display flight and/or train (Amtrak) status.

If you turn on Arrival & Departure Status on the main (or home) site, it appears on both the Log In page and Home page of the main site. If you turn on Arrival & Departure Status on a subsite, it appears on the subsite’s Home page.

To configure the display of arrival and departure status

  1. To display the arrival and departure status, select the Show Arrival & Departure Status check box.
  2. Select whether you want to display the status:
    • On the Login and Home pages
    • On the Log In page only
    • On the Home page only

  3. Select whether you want to Include Train Status.

    Note  If you have enabled Amtrak on your site, you can show arrival and departure status for Amtrak.

  4. Enter the custom message you want displayed on the top of the Arrival & Departure Status Results section. There is no default message.
  5. Enter the custom message you want displayed below the Arrival & Departure Status Results section. There is no default message.
  6. Click Save.
