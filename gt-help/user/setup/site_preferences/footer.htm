Footer

Path: Setup > Site Preferences > Footer

You can use the Footer page display the Privacy Policy link in the footer.

 To configure the privacy policy setting

  1. Select whether to Show the Privacy Policy link in the footer.
  2. In the URL box, enter the URL for the Privacy Policy link.
  3. Click Save.
