Service Fees

Path: Setup > Site Preferences > Service Fees

You can use the Service Fees page to control the service fees that you can charge to an airline ticket.

For more information, click one of the following:

Setup Service Fees

  1. Select Do you want to enable Service Fees on this site. You can select Yes, airline service fee, Yes, trip service fee, or No. The default is No. If you select No, the system does not display any type of service fee (airline, trip, or custom).

    If you selected...

    Enter or select the...

    Airline Service Fees

    1. Enter the Service Fee field label.
    2. Enter the Service Fee monetary amount.
    3. Select If the site is configured for Air Connect, should the default Service Fee apply to Air Connect Itineraries?
    4. Continue below to add a custom service fee.

    Trip Service Fees

    1. For executives, in the Trip Fee - Executive Online row, do one of the following:

      If you want to

      Then

      Charge an executive service fee

      1. In the Status list, click Fee amount.
      2. In the Service Fee Charge box, type the service fee amount.

      Waive the executive service fee

      In the Status list, click Waive fee.

      Not have an executive service fee

      In the Status list, click No fee.

    2. For other travelers, in the Trip Fee - Online Reservation row, do one of the following:

      If you want to

      Then

      Charge a service fee

      1. In the Status list, click Fee amount.
      2. In the Service Fee Charge box, type the service fee amount.

      Waive the service fee

      In the Status list, click Waive fee.

      Not have a service fee

      In the Status list, click No fee.

  2. Click Save.

Add a Custom Service Fee

  1. In the Custom Service Fees area, click Add.
  2. Enter the Service Fee field label.
  3. Select Which airlines should have this Service Fee applied. To select multiple items, hold down the Ctrl key while you click other items that you want to select.
  4. Enter the Service Fee monetary amount.
  5. Select If the site is configured for Air Connect, should the default Service Fee apply to Air Connect Itineraries. The default setting is No.
  6. Click Save.

Edit a Custom Service Fee

  1. In the Custom Service Fees table, in the Label column, click the service fee name you want to edit.
  2. Edit the custom service fee information as needed.
  3. Click Save.

Delete a Custom Service Fee

  1. In the Custom Service Fees table, select the check boxes for the custom service fees you want to delete.

  2. Click Delete.

    A confirmation dialog box appears.

  3. Click OK.

Delete all Custom Service Fees

  1. Select the check box in the header row.

  2. Click Delete.

    A confirmation dialog box appears.

  3. Click OK.
