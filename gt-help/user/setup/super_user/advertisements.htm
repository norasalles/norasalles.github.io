Advertisements

Path: Setup > Super User > Advertisements

PromoSpots on GetThere give travel managers another merchandising tool to promote their preferred programs offering clear, relevant and company-endorsed supplier options to travelers.

This feature helps increase brand awareness around a company’s preferred suppliers. This visual “aid” has proven to increase hotel and car attachment rates, increase online adoption, and reduce offline booking costs.

A traveler or traveler arranger making a booking on their GetThere site is presented with preferred supplier promotions specific to their points of travel.

Notes   

  • Advertisements is disabled by default.
  • Once enabled, ensure your Corporate Vendor Markets in Site Administration are updated with your preferred suppliers.

To configure advertisements

  1. Select whether to Enable Advertisements for this site.
  2. If you enable advertisements, select the Maximum number of ad displayed. This is currently set to 3 and cannot be changed.
  3. In the Select the subsite(s) that ad will display list, select the subsites in which you want to show advertisements. To select multiple items, hold down the Ctrl key while you click other items that you want to select.
  4. Click Save.
