If you did not find what you were searching for, please check for the following:

Did you use a natural language search?

For example, did you search for something like "How do I save my trip as a template"? If so, the Help system searched for "How" and "do" and "I" and "save" and "my" and "flight" and "as" and "a" and "template". The system has to find all the words in a topic in any order before it displays the topic in the search results. Since all of those words do not appear in any topic, the search results show "0". Instead, you could search for "trip template" and you would find the information you are looking for.

Did you search for a location, a hotel, a PNR, or other trip information?

This Help only contains instructions for how to use the system. It does not contain information on specific flights, hotels, destinations, companies or businesses, or any other trip information.

Did you check your spelling?

A common reason for not finding anything is a simple misspelling.

Are you looking for contact information?

Support or help desk contacts or phone numbers are not stored in this Help system. This information is managed by your company or your travel management company.

Are you looking for information on a specific error?

Most error messages contain instructions for what to do about the problem. However, if you need additional help, please contact your corporate travel department. If you are not sure who to contact, you may find support contact information at the bottom of your travel booking site. This Help system does not contain specific error messages or troubleshooting information.

Have you checked the comments in related Help topics?

Most likely, you are not the first person to look for your question. Check the comments shown in any related Help topics.

Are looking for instructions not related to anything mentioned previously, and still cannot find what you are looking for?

Please use the feedback box at the bottom of any Help topic to let us know what you are looking for.
