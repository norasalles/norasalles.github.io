Trams Back Office 4.10 Beta New Features and Enhancements

Enhancements made between Trams Back Office 4.09.00.032 and 4.10.00.002

Download this enhancement if your agency uses the Merchant Credit Card Processing in Trams Back Office. You may ignore this enhancement if you do not use the processing utility.
